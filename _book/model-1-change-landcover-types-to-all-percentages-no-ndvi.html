8.1 MODEL 1: Change landcover types to all percentages, no NDVI

ABOUT THIS MODEL - stream samples during peak flow to upper descending (May - July25) n=30 - response is LABILE.

Our indicators for each latent variable are as follows:
Topo:
–mean distance that allochthonous water has to travel to the stream channel
–mean aspect in radians
–mean subcatchment TWI
Landcover:
– % of subcatchment pixels with harvested/regenerating vegetation – mean NDVI
LABILE:
